An agentic AI framework for automated photonics inverse design. Given a target optical specification, the system orchestrates optimization agents that explore the design space and converge on manufacturable device geometries — all on a single GPU.

### Key Highlights
- **Agentic Optimization**: Deploys intelligent agents that iteratively propose, simulate, and refine photonic structures to meet target performance specs.
- **Multi-Objective Design**: Supports simultaneous optimization of multiple optical figures of merit (transmission, bandwidth, extinction ratio).
- **Single-GPU Execution**: Engineered for accessibility — runs full inverse design pipelines without requiring HPC clusters.
