Neuro-Mesh is a distributed multi-agent AI runtime designed to let multiple users' mini-models collaborate together at very low cost, iterating through multiple reasoning rounds until converging on a correct answer.

### Key Highlights
- **Multi-User Collaboration**: Connects separate users' AI agents into a shared mesh network for cooperative problem solving.
- **Iterative Convergence**: Agents exchange intermediate reasoning and refine answers over multiple iterations until consensus is reached.
- **Cost Efficiency**: Optimized for low-cost inference by distributing computation across lightweight models rather than relying on a single large model.
