An academic research project at Georgia Tech focusing on designing robust reinforcement learning agents that perform automated trading decisions under highly dynamic, non-stationary market conditions.

### Key Highlights

- **Policy Optimization**: Formulated custom reward functions based on risk-adjusted return metrics (Sharpe/Sortino ratios) to guide policy training.
- **Non-Stationary Adaptation**: Developed exploration strategies that adjust online to shifting market trends, mitigating policy collapse during sudden market shocks.
- **Evaluation**: Simulating trading sessions on historical equity data, proving superior resilience compared to standard Q-learning and heuristic baselines.
