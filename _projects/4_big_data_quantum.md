A Vertically Integrated Project (VIP) at the Georgia Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Andrew J Medford. The research focused on utilizing advanced deep learning models to predict quantum mechanical properties of chemical structures.

### Key Highlights
- **DFT Calculations**: Executed high-throughput Density Functional Theory (DFT) simulations using Quantum Espresso to calculate surface adsorption energies.
- **Equiformer_v2 Graph Neural Network**: Evaluated and benchmarked advanced Equivariant Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) against classical ML baselines for material property predictions.
- **Data Wrangling**: Managed massive datasets of atomistic simulations, optimizing database access and ingestion for model training.
